pAIgeBreaker™
The textbook is dead.

PaigeBreaker — AI Textbook Replacement

Founded and built a system of 7 coordinated AI agents that replace the static textbook with a living, personalized curriculum — generated from a syllabus upload in seconds.

Agentic Pipeline: Outliner → Generator → Assessor → Scorer → Persona Router → Mastery Gate → Remediator. Each agent handles a discrete function; together they produce a chapter personalized to each student's name, major, career goals, and learning history.

Architecture: Learning Genome™ (portable student identity), adaptive difficulty via real-time mastery signals, Socratic assessments, and institutional audit queues. LTI 1.3 integrated, FERPA & HIPAA ready, Bloom's & DOK tagged.

Impact: 70% cost reduction vs. legacy publishers. 90-second chapter generation. Disrupting a $26B market — one syllabus at a time.
