FAQ

Questions answered

What is MoodScript AI?
MoodScript AI is an AI-powered journaling app that lets you capture your thoughts via voice or text, then instantly analyses your mood, detects emotions, and delivers personalized coaching insights. Think of it as a therapist-grade journaling companion in your pocket.
Which AI model does it use?
You choose! MoodScript AI supports OpenAI (GPT-4o mini), Google Gemini (1.5 Flash), and Anthropic Claude (Haiku). You can switch between them anytime in Settings and provide your own API key for unlimited usage.
Is my journal private and secure?
Absolutely. Your entries are encrypted at rest in Firebase (Google Cloud) and only accessible to you. We never read, sell, or share your journal data with third parties. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time.
